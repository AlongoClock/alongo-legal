Alongo
Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 5, 2026

Alongo is a couples' alarm app that lets you wake each other up with voice messages. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

We do not require your email address, phone number, or real name to use Alongo.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

We use anonymous authentication to create a unique account identifier. No email, phone number, or personal identity is required.

Data We Store

  • Anonymous account identifier
  • Display name (a randomly generated nickname you can customize)
  • Pair relationship information (including your paired user's anonymous identifier)
  • Voice messages (securely stored in Cloud Firestore for delivery between paired users)
  • Account creation and activity timestamps

What We Do Not Collect

Alongo does not collect or access:

  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your photos or media files
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Your SMS messages

Device Permissions

The following permissions are used by Alongo:

Permission Purpose
Microphone
(RECORD_AUDIO)		 Record voice messages only when you choose to send one.
Notifications
(POST_NOTIFICATIONS)		 Deliver alarm notifications.
Full Screen Intent
(USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT)		 Display the alarm screen while ringing.
Exact Alarm
(SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM)		 Wake you at the scheduled time.
Boot Completed
(RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED)		 Restore alarms after device restart.
Vibrate
(VIBRATE)		 Vibrate during alarms.
Wake Lock
(WAKE_LOCK)		 Keep the device awake while an alarm is ringing.
Foreground Service
(FOREGROUND_SERVICE + FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE)		 Run the alarm ringing service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your anonymous account
  • To pair your account with your partner's account
  • To send and receive voice messages as alarm sounds
  • To schedule and deliver alarms at exact times
  • To restore alarms after device reboots

3. Data Retention

Your account data and voice messages remain stored while your account remains active.

When you unpair from your partner, shared voice messages stored on our servers are removed as part of the unpairing process. Some information may remain in backup systems or temporary technical logs for a limited period where required for security, legal, or operational purposes. We make reasonable efforts to remove eligible data as described in this Privacy Policy. Voice messages temporarily stored on your own device remain under your control and may persist until removed by the application, your device's cache management, or by uninstalling the app.

Because Alongo uses anonymous authentication, uninstalling the app may create a new anonymous account when you reinstall. Data associated with a previous anonymous account may remain on our servers and may no longer be accessible to you. To request deletion of data associated with a previous anonymous account, please contact us at alongoapp@gmail.com.

Some information may remain temporarily stored on your device to support offline functionality and improve app performance. This locally cached data is managed by your device and may be removed when the app cache is cleared or the app is uninstalled.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Firebase Authentication — Anonymous sign-in only
  • Cloud Firestore — Data storage for account info, pair relationships, and voice messages

Firebase processes data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

At this time, Alongo does not use:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Payment SDKs

If we introduce analytics or other data processing services in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Your Rights

  • You may change your display name at any time
  • You may unpair from your partner. As part of the unpairing process, shared voice messages stored on our servers are removed.
  • You may request deletion of your account data by contacting alongoapp@gmail.com
  • You may deny or revoke any device permission at any time through your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

Alongo is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it as soon as reasonably practicable.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Access to user data is restricted to authorized systems required to operate the service.

No method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States, where Google Firebase operates its infrastructure. We take reasonable measures to protect your data during these transfers.

This Privacy Policy is governed by applicable law.
